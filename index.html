Emilio Stanley Mwai Kibaki

Emilio Stanley Mwai Kibaki (15 November 1931 – 21 April 2022) better known as Mwai Kibaki, was a Kenyan politician who served as the third President of Kenya from December 2002 until April 2013.

  • He had previously served as the fourth Vice-President of Kenya for ten years from 1978 to 1988 under President Daniel arap Moi. He also held cabinet ministerial positions in the Jomo Kenyatta and Daniel arap Moi governments, including as minister for Finance (1969–1981) under Kenyatta, and Minister for Home Affairs (1982–1988) and Minister for Health (1988–1991) under Moi

  • On 29 December 2002, still nursing injuries from the motor vehicle accident and in a wheel chair, Mwai Kibaki was sworn in as the third President and Commander in Chief of the Armed Forces of the Republic of Kenya,in front of thousands of cheering supporters at the historic Uhuru Park within Nairobi City.
